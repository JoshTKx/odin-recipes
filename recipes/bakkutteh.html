Bak Kut Teh

Description

Bak Kut Teh in the Hokkien or Fujianese dialect literally translates to Pork Rib Tea. This rich herbal soup contains dong gui (Angelica Sinensis), known for its warming properties. For this reason, it is popularly eaten as a supper or late night meal in Malaysia and Singapore.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole rack of spare ribs
  • 3 tbsp white peppercorns
  • 3 star anise
  • cinnamon
  • 15 cloves of garlic
  • 225g tofu puffs
  • 2tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2tbsp firsh sauce
  • chillies
  • light soy sauce
